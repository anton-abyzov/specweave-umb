## First comment from maker

Hey Product Hunt — Anton here, sole maintainer of vSkill.

I built this after watching teams ship AI agent skills as copy-pasted folders, with no install
command, no version control, and no way to prove a skill made the model any better. vSkill is
a real package manager for AI skills, with a three-tier security scan baked in (52 static
patterns + author blocklist + LLM-intent check) and a built-in A/B eval harness so you can
benchmark across Claude, GPT, Llama, and Gemini side by side.

The desktop app is fully native — Tauri shell + Node SEA sidecar — with a 1.5 s cold launch
and a global Cmd-K palette. Auto-update on every platform, signed manifest, zero telemetry.

It's MIT-licensed, runs locally, and works with 53 agent platforms (Claude Code, Cursor,
Copilot, Codex, Windsurf, Zed, Gemini CLI, Ollama, LM Studio…). I'd love your feedback on
the eval harness in particular — that's the part I think most teams will actually use.
