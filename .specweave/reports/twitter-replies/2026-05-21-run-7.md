## What worked this run (technique notes)

- `form_input` fails on X's Lexical composer (`<div contenteditable>`). Use `document.execCommand('insertText', false, text)` after focusing the composer — works first try.
- Submit button is `[data-testid="tweetButtonInline"]` for the inline reply composer on a tweet detail page.
- `composer.innerText === "\n"` after submit is the success signal (X clears the composer on accepted post).
- Visiting `https://x.com/search?q=from:aabyzov&f=live` is the reliable way to confirm posts — `/with_replies` lagged ~5 min and only showed 3 of 6 initially.
