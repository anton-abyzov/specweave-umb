## Technique notes (for future runs)

- The "Post your reply" placeholder click sometimes opens the inline composer in dialog mode (full modal) and sometimes inline. When it's modal, click the body field at (780, 275) before typing. When inline, type directly.
- After typing, the Reply button color shifts from gray to black/blue. Wait 1-2s before clicking for safety.
- "Your post was sent" toast appears bottom-center for ~5s after success.
- `Array.from(document.querySelectorAll('article')).slice(0,N).map(a => a.querySelector('a[href*="/aabyzov/status/"]').href)` reliably grabs the URLs of the most recent N own posts when on the from:aabyzov live search page.
