## Notes for future runs (technique)

- `document.execCommand('insertText', false, text)` after `composer.focus()` works on the Lexical contenteditable. Run 7 finding holds.
- **First-call duplication gotcha**: if you click into the composer THEN execCommand from JS, the placeholder text "Post your reply" may already be active and the insertText appends. Fix: `selectAll` + `delete` before insertText, or skip the click and focus only via JS.
- `[data-testid="tweetButtonInline"]` for inline reply composer.
- Success signal: `composer.innerText` returns `"\n"` or empty string after submit + "Your post was sent" toast appears at bottom.
- URL of the just-posted reply: scrape `a[href*="/aabyzov/status/"]` from page and take the highest numeric status ID — newer IDs are larger.
