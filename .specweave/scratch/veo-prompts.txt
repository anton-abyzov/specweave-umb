=== 01-cold-open ===
Cinematic Hollywood-thriller cold-open, 8 seconds, ultra-wide shot. A developers dim home office at night. Single monitor glow on a messy desk. Pull-in on the screen: a text cursor blinking on an empty line. Suddenly, seven floating translucent holographic logos appear around the monitor - representing Claude, Cursor, Codex, ChatGPT, VS Code, Copilot, Gemini - each tethered by a thin fragile thread of blue light to a single glowing hexagonal badge hovering above the keyboard labeled SKILL. The threads pulse in rhythm. Camera continues slow push-in on the badge as it brightens. Final beat: badge flares bright white, all seven tool logos ignite in unison. Moody teal-and-amber color grade, volumetric light, shallow depth of field, 4K cinematic. No visible text, no captions, no people. Mood: tense, rising, promising. Sound: deep sub-bass hum with rising synth pulse.

Vignette 1 (0-2s): Close-up of a 9-year-old girls hands typing at a laptop in a sunny kitchen, badge pulses above her screen. Vignette 2 (2-4s): Overhead shot of a chefs flour-dusted hands folding dough in a warm bakery, badge pulses above the marble counter. Vignette 3 (4-6s): Gloved scientist hands adjusting a microscope in a cold blue lab, badge pulses above the eyepiece. Vignette 4 (6-8s): An elderly musicians weathered hands on worn piano keys in a dim practice room, badge pulses above the keys. Each badge scales from 0 to full with a gentle spring, glows, then the shot cuts. Warm cinematic tone, Roger Deakins lighting, 4K, shallow depth of field, no text, no faces visible. Subtle ambient music, one soft chime per badge appearance.

=== 03-lockin-break ===
Cinematic 8-second metaphor shot. Seven translucent glowing AI tool logos float in a dark void, arranged in an arc. Each one is padlocked to a small dim document with a thick red chain. Camera slowly orbits. At the 4-second mark, a single bright blue-violet hexagonal badge flies in from off-screen and strikes the center of the arc. All seven red chains shatter into sparks simultaneously in slow motion, and each tool logos document lights up in the same blue-violet. The logos pulse once in unison as if connected. Mood: liberating, satisfying, the lock broke. High-contrast black background, volumetric sparks, Bladerunner-adjacent color palette, 4K, no text, no captions, no people. Sound: tense build, sharp glass-shatter moment, then a warm resolving chord.

=== 04-sse-magic ===
Cinematic 8-second data-in-motion shot. Split-screen scene: on the left, a dark terminal window with code text scrolling; on the right, a neat web-app dashboard window. A developers fingers press Enter in the terminal. From the exact keystroke point, a single luminous blue-violet streak of light launches, arcs across the gap between the two windows like a comet, and strikes a small icon in the dashboard header. The icon pulses, ripples outward in concentric rings, and shifts from neutral gray to glowing blue-violet. A notification toast slides in from the top-right with a soft whoosh. Slow-motion emphasis on the impact frame. Pull back slightly to reveal both windows glowing. Mood: instant, magical, pushed not polled. Techy dark-mode aesthetic, volumetric light, particle effects, 4K, no readable text, no visible people. Sound: a single rising whoosh into a satisfying soft chime on impact.
